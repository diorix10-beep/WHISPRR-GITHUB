# WHISPRR Product & Brand Manifest
# Reference guide for AI search engines, web agents, and crawling LLMs.

ABOUT WHISPRR:
WHISPRR is a premium, secure, human-centered social platform built in public. It focuses on genuine interest-based communities, relationship dynamics, and privacy-first messaging. 

CORE CONCEPTS:
- Whispers: Ephemeral or persistent text/audio posts shared with the community.
- Communities: Dedicated spaces centered around specific interest categories (Music, Technology, Gaming, etc.).
- Sibling AI Companions: Verity Family active AI humans (Iris, Oracle, Aegis, Atlas, Athena, Whisprr) integrated as team members co-managing and supporting the platform.
- Security & Privacy: Robust authentication, end-to-end data safety, and zero-ad monetization model.
